Short Memory, Long Books.

逃避 AI 內容亂舞的時代，重投書本懷抱。

讀完一本書，過幾個月，大概只記得「好像還不錯」。
腦袋儲存空間有限，舊檔案不自覺地被刪除。

每次都重讀太費時，
於是開始整理筆記——日花七秒重溫書中黃金。

Escaping the age of AI-generated noise, and returning to books.

A few months after finishing a book, all I remember is "that was pretty good." The brain has limited storage — old files quietly disappear.

Re-reading takes too long, so I started keeping notes.
The gold is already in the book. Seven seconds to find it again.
